I am a research engineer at Orange Labs and a PhD candidate at Université Panthéon-Sorbonne.
I work with Pr Fabrice Rossi and Dr. Marc Boullé on the exploratory analysis of data streams. More specifically, we are looking for ways of building compact yet reliable summaries of volatile data

I hold a double master degree on Computer Science & Engineering from TELECOM Nancy and on Artifical Intelligence from Université de Lorraine.

Other than machine learning and statistics, I spend my time doing abstract drawings

This page is still under construction, but feel free to browse around !

I'm interested in machine learning, optimization and infomation theory.
Much of my current research is focused on extracting interesting information from large amounts of data using the Minimum Description Length principle (MDL), an information theory framework for model selection.
